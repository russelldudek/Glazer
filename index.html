Why this role matters now

The operating surface is widening.

Southern Glazer's combines national distribution, connected logistics, digital commerce, market intelligence, and a changing commercial model. The hiring problem is not merely producing more models. It is making production AI dependable enough to influence consequential work.

Boundary: Internal use cases and operating assumptions are hypotheses for discovery, not claims about Southern Glazer's roadmap.

Operating model

The Model Reliability Contract

Choose a production event. The system reveals the signal, accountable owner, intervention choice, and evidence required to return to service.

Hold, diagnose, and invoke named authority.

Signal
Drift crosses the contract threshold.
Owner
Model owner with business and platform partners.
Action
Tune, retrain, rollback, fallback, or accept a bounded exception.
Return evidence
Evaluation renewed; outcome and risk rechecked.

Protect the workflow, then remove recurring toil.

Signal
Latency or availability breaches the service envelope.
Owner
Platform or service owner.
Action
Fallback, scale, simplify, or redesign the serving path.
Return evidence
SLO restored and recovery path exercised.

Do not optimize a model away from the work.

Signal
Technical health is stable while adoption or business outcome decays.
Owner
Business outcome owner with product and data science.
Action
Reframe objective, redesign workflow, retrain, or stop.
Return evidence
Workflow adoption and outcome recover.

Restore meaning before restoring service.

Signal
Schema, lineage, freshness, or semantic contract changes.
Owner
Data owner with model and platform owners.
Action
Quarantine, repair, revalidate, then release.
Return evidence
Data contract and model evaluation both renewed.

Hard objection

The qualification gap should be discussed, not disguised.

Posted: seven years of ML platform operations and four years leading that function.

Verified: director-level technical and operating leadership, high-reliability quality systems, 24/7 mechanisms, telemetry-to-action loops, AI-first transformation, and AI workflow governance.

The hiring question is whether Southern Glazer's needs a deep platform specialist in the director chair, or an operator-technologist who can build the reliability system around a technically strong MLOps organization.

Location constraint: The posting requires residence near Miramar or Dallas. Russell's verified location is Pittsburgh; this campaign does not imply relocation intent.
