Operating model

The Model Reliability Contract

Choose a production event. The system reveals the signal, accountable owner, intervention choice, and evidence required to return to service.

Hold, diagnose, and invoke named authority.

Signal
Drift crosses the contract threshold.
Owner
Model owner with business and platform partners.
Action
Tune, retrain, rollback, fallback, or accept a bounded exception.
Return evidence
Evaluation renewed; outcome and risk rechecked.

Protect the workflow, then remove recurring toil.

Signal
Latency or availability breaches the service envelope.
Owner
Platform/service owner.
Action
Fallback, scale, simplify, or redesign the serving path.
Return evidence
SLO restored and recovery path exercised.

Do not optimize a model away from the work.

Signal
Technical health remains stable while adoption or business outcome decays.
Owner
Business outcome owner with product and data science.
Action
