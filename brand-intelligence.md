# Southern Glazer's Brand and Asset Intelligence

## Source boundary

This record uses current public Southern Glazer's corporate, Proof, supply-chain, careers, and leadership pages. It is not an official brand guide. Campaign colors and motion are employer-adjacent interpretations, not claimed official specifications.

## Compact brand record

- **Official logo source:** `https://www.southernglazers.com/content/dam/web/qa/southern-glazers-logo-secondary.svg`
- **Compact/mobile logo source:** `https://www.southernglazers.com/content/dam/web/qa/southern-glazers-logo-secondary-mobile.svg`
- **Palette:** public heritage cues suggest burgundy/wine, warm gold/copper, deep ink, white, and cream. Campaign tokens are `#722D40`, `#B85E2D`, `#E8A653`, `#171514`, `#F5EEE5`, and white. These are not represented as official published values.
- **Typography character:** editorial heritage serif for authority and premium positioning, paired with a direct sans serif for operating detail and digital clarity. No official typeface is claimed.
- **Photography/illustration character:** beverage and hospitality context balanced with people, customers, warehouses, fleets, logistics, and digital commerce.
- **Geometry and interface motifs:** circular heritage forms, fine linework, warm metallic accents, editorial whitespace, structured commerce panels, and high-contrast operational information.
- **Motion character:** the public digital presence reads as calm, polished, and precise rather than playful. Campaign motion therefore uses measured mechanical state changes, short transitions, and controlled signal movement.
- **Overall vibe:** premium heritage + national operating scale + modern commercial intelligence + physical-distribution credibility.
- **Approved or supportable assets:** public facts, linked source pages, and the candidate-created `assets/reliability-mark.svg`.
- **Original visuals created:** Reliability Interlock, signal track, operating-contract scenario transitions, handoff corridor, and scorecard ribbon.
- **Recommended use by artifact:** expressive burgundy/gold and motion on the candidate site; restrained accents and white canvas in resume and cover letter; line-based consulting-report treatment in briefs and operating artifacts.

## Asset decision

The campaign does not embed the official company logo. No campaign-use permission or complete public usage guide was identified, and omission reduces endorsement or impersonation risk. The Reliability Interlock and mark are original candidate-created graphics.
