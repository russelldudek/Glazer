## Typography evidence and decision

The public identity uses a heritage/editorial contrast between expressive brand lettering and clean operational copy. An official, reusable font family and licensing permission could not be verified from publicly parsed page content. No proprietary webfont files were downloaded or committed.

Implementation:
- Display: Georgia / Times New Roman fallback to reproduce editorial authority and contrast.
- Body: Segoe UI / Trebuchet MS / Arial fallback for direct operational readability.
- The substitutes are not represented as official. Fidelity is carried through hierarchy, weight, tracking, line height, case, restrained density, and serif/sans contrast.

## Photography, product, facility, and interface cues

Public Southern Glazer's imagery balances beverage/hospitality context with people, customers, warehouses, fleets, logistics, and digital commerce. No official photography is embedded because a clear publication/reuse context for specific files was not established. The campaign instead creates original supporting visuals from documented company cues:
- red/gold heritage identity;
- warm editorial fields and fine rules;
- distribution-network scale;
- connected logistics and route-planning structure;
- Proof-like digital commerce density without copying proprietary UI;
- precise, calm motion rather than playful effects.
